FAQ

What do the colored badges mean?

Each candidate carries one of three short-list integrity badges:

  • Clean — no major item on our short public-issues list. Verify anyway.
  • Public issues — controversies or legal/ethics matters widely reported in mainstream PH media.
  • Serious legal — the highest tier (e.g. ICC scrutiny). Not a court ruling.

Where does the news come from?

BayanCheck aggregates Philippine news live from public RSS feeds — Rappler, Inquirer, ABS-CBN News, GMA, Manila Bulletin, Philstar, Vera Files, Bilyonaryo, plus GDELT 2.0 (filtered to Philippine sources). Every headline links back to its original outlet. We don't republish full articles; we link out so you can read at the source.

How are Issues & Projects filled in?

Each candidate's Issues, Projects, Recent Coverage, and Multi-platform Mentions sections are auto-populated from Wikipedia + GDELT + Reddit at page load. Sections marked AUTO are structured automatically; every claim has a "Sources" footer linking to where it came from. If a section is empty, that means the public sources don't have it yet — please continue to verify.

How are Senate hearing dates tracked?

The "Upcoming Senate Hearings" panel on each senator's profile is scraped live from the official Senate of the Philippines committee calendar (web.senate.gov.ph/committee/calendar.asp) and matched against the senator's name. Refreshes every hour.

What is BayanBot?

BayanBot is the floating chat assistant in the bottom-right corner. It's a strictly-guarded AI helper that ONLY answers questions about Philippine politics, government, and elections. It cites a source URL for every factual claim and refuses to endorse or rank candidates.

It works out of the box for free — by default it uses Pollinations (a free, no-key, no-signup AI service). Just click the 💬 button and start asking questions.

If you want better quality, click ⚙ Settings and switch to one of these (all also free):

  • Google Gemini — free key, no credit card needed.
  • Groq · Llama 3.1 70B — free key, very fast.
  • Anthropic Claude or OpenAI GPT-4o — paid, optional.

All API keys are stored only in your browser. More on privacy →

Is the AI 100% unbiased?

No AI is. We mitigate by (1) hard-coding a "neutral, no endorsements, cite every claim" system prompt, (2) restricting BayanBot to facts in the loaded context (Wikipedia + PH news + Senate records), and (3) flagging if it ever cites a non-Philippine source. Always verify against the cited links.

Why no Facebook integration?

Meta's terms of service forbid scraping, and the third-party scraper APIs that try anyway return what the candidate's PR team chose to post — which is the opposite of unbiased. If you want official Facebook page integration we'll need a Meta Developer App + Page Access Token; without that, we use Reddit + YouTube + the wider news net to surface multi-platform mentions.

Are allegations the same as convictions?

No. Each issue is labeled with its status (allegation / pending / under investigation / hearing / dismissed / resolved / convicted) and links to the original public source.
